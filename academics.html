Student Evaluation

How we track progress

Assessment at Hana Bloom Academy is continuous, multi-modal, and focused on genuine mastery — not performance for a single high-stakes test.

  • Unit tests and chapter reviews after each completed section
  • Writing portfolios maintained across the academic year
  • Oral recitation and Socratic discussion as regular assessments
  • Annual standardized assessments as benchmarking tools
  • Parent-maintained transcripts and course completion records
  • Real-world project evaluations and skill demonstrations
  • Quarterly family reviews to assess pace, gaps, and direction
